## Overview
This guide will walk you through the steps to navigate from the Agenda Distribution page back to the original Meeting page within your business application. Whether you need to adjust meeting details or review other agenda items, these steps will help you make that transition smoothly.

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Open The Original Meeting Page
1. Locate your meeting ID.
2. Access the meeting page by using the `meetingPage` and entering the `activeMeetingId` associated with your meeting.

### Go To The Agenda Distribution
1. Navigate to the option that allows you to send reports, labeled as "Send Report".
2. Wait for the system to load the Meetings Send Agenda page corresponding to your current meeting.

### Access The Sending Agenda Page
1. Open the `sendAgendaPage` by again using the `activeMeetingId` to ensure you are on the right agenda.

### Return To The Meeting Page
1. Use the "Go Back" option on the `sendAgendaPage` to initiate the return to the Meeting page.
2. Wait for the Meetings Meeting Page to load properly for your active meeting.

### Close The Agenda Page
1. Once on the Meetings Meeting Page, you can exit the `sendAgendaPage` by selecting the close option. 

By following these steps, you will successfully navigate back to the original Meeting page from the Agenda Distribution section.
